Personal Introduction
======
Qingtian Wu is a Lecturer in the School of Artificial Intelligence, Shenzhen Polytechnic University. In April 2024, I got my doctoral degree from [Faculty of Science and Technology](https://www.fst.um.edu.mo/), [University of Macau](https://www.um.edu.mo/), with research interests in Computer Vision and Intelligent Systems. I also have an internship at Guangming Laboratory,  supervised by [Prof. Fei Richard Yu](https://csse.szu.edu.cn/pages/user/index?id=1199).
